MISSION E2L

As we already know that due to COVID-19, we all have suffered greatly. Being trapped in your own house and and not being able to do anything for your self seems like a house arrest. Therefore, we a group of Students have come togather and have built this platform. Our Mission here at ETL is to provide you guys a safe, cheap and judgement free environment where you can have diffrent work outs at home and the best part about all this is that you dont even need any fancy equipment. So, come join us in this wonderful journey where we help each other out, and tackle the lockdown togather.
